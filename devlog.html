July 30th, 2025 - What's in store for JoltNotes


Welcome to my first devlog for JotNotes, I thought I could start something like this to one, shorten my release nots for updates on JoltNotes, but to also express my options somewhere. I just released v0.2.1 of JoltNotes, removing the most important function for a notes app...


The menu button


I removed this due to the fact that I did not understand how to add this function. All that button did was bring a new menu up that said settings and notes. I had no clue how to add notes to that menu, or even add notes to the users computer storage to store sevreal notes. But I will bring that back, however it might not be very soon.

This update is the first offical release of a stable build, not a alpha or beta. So I don't plan to release another for a bit, I'm a bit slow on releasing any updates. I will be starting development on v0.3.0-alpha.0 starting tomorrow, mainly brainstorming ideas of what I could add or remove. In the meantime I will be updating the stable build of 0.2.1, fixing anything thats broken and refining the version, making it perfect.

This is it for me for now, but I do plan to write soon. This will be a devlog for everything I work on, the title will show what I'm talking about.
