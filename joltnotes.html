JoltNotes: Your Focus-First Note-Taking App


JoltNotes is a simple, minimalist note-taking application designed to keep you focused. Available for both macOS and Windows, it features a single, expansive text box that fills almost the entire window, eliminating distractions and putting your content front and center. Forget about saving – JoltNotes auto-saves your work instantly after just one second of inactivity, ensuring your thoughts are always secure. Plus, your notes load immediately when you launch the app, seamlessly picking up right where you left off.

Experience Uninterrupted Writing

JoltNotes is built to get out of your way and let you focus on what truly matters: your ideas. The main application window offers an ultra-minimalist writing environment.

The Intuitive JoltNotes Toolbar

The JoltNotes toolbar is designed for instant access to essential tools and settings, keeping your note-taking experience smooth and efficient.

Personalize Your Workspace: Colorful Themes

Beyond functionality, JoltNotes lets you express your style. Discover a diverse range of colorful themes designed to customize the aesthetic of your notes and set the perfect mood for your work.
